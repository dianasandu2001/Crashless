Tutorial page

The game starts in Helsinki Vantaa Airport. From here you can go visit any of the randomly generated airports in Europe. Do keep in mind that there are only 4 airports generated from each country and every time you move to a new airport new ones generate.

The goal of the game is for you to visit 5 different countries without running out of fuel. To help you, we have a country specific question each time you reach an airport that can award you extra fuel to help you reach your goal.

Your name, difficulty, fuel consumed, fuel left (budget), and how many countries you have visited so far are shown on the right of the map, in the game status box. (as show in the picture)

Good luck and have fun! :D
